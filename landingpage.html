ABOUT US

Thane Festival is one of India's biggest art festival, conducted every year to celebrate our rich heritage of art and entertainment, drawing art enthusiasts and cognoscenti in very large numbers.

Exquisite paintings and soulful music to rhapsody dance moves, the event hosts a plethora of events to beguile everyone from striplings to gold agers.

Darshan Raval is an Indian singer, composer, and songwriter. He is known for his work in Hindi, Gujarati, Punjabi and Bengali. He was runner-up in the 2014 StarPlus reality music show India's Raw Star
