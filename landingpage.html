ABOUT US

Thane Festival is one of India's biggest art festival, conducted every year to celebrate our rich heritage of art and entertainment, drawing art enthusiasts and cognoscenti in very large numbers.Exquisite paintings and soulful music to rhapsody dance moves, the event hosts a plethora of events to beguile everyone from striplings to gold agers.

The event is designed to engage a wide demographic, offering a plethora of activities and exhibitions. Young attendees (striplings) can find inspiration and joy in creative workshops and interactive exhibits, while older visitors (gold agers) can indulge in the nostalgia and beauty of traditional and contemporary art forms. The Thane Festival is not just an event but a celebration of India's artistic spirit, promising an unforgettable experience for every visitor.
